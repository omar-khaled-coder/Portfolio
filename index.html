About me

Hello My name is Omar, and I was born in Egypt and raised in Saudi Arabia. I started my journey as a professional photographer at the age of 17, which I continued for over a decade. After graduating from Sinai University as a dentist, I transitioned into digital marketing by learning Udacity's nano degree program in the field. I then worked as a social media marketer specialist and content creator.

Due to my experience as a photographer, I was able to work for a real estate company and as a freelancer on notable projects such as the Pepsi Ramadan campaign and Hilton Cairo campaign. I have always been interested in coding and web development, which led me to pursue Udacity's nano degree program in full-stack web development. After completing the program, I joined Le Wagon's full-stack web development program to learn even more.

I am passionate about learning and always seeking to acquire new skills and knowledge. In addition to my interest in full-stack web development, I am also keen on learning devops. Alongside my professional pursuits, my hobbies include problem-solving, photography, scuba diving, and swimming.
Overall, my life story is one of pursuing my passions and interests, embracing change, and seeking growth through continuous learning.
